India is the world's largest country - it's no wonder that it houses the world's second-largest movie industry, Bollywood. According to WifiTalents, the industry puts out over one thousand movies a year. At this point, almost everyone has heard something about the dramatic nature of these movies or seen a few clips on the internet. However, what everyone does not know is that the Indian movie industry spans beyond Bollywood. As stated by the Indian Government, there are 22 major languages recognized in the country of India, of which only one is Hindi. There are 28 states in India. Hindi movies are Bollywood films, but there are also Tamil Kollywood films, and Telugu Tollywood films, and so on. These industries are usually localized by states, as almost every state has its own language. Many people are unaware of these smaller industries and the diversity of Indian cinema, so this website goes in depth about a select few.
